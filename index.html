Campus Life

school grounds

remodelled in 2018

The school grounds provide space for several sports and extra-curricular activities. CHIREC has two synthetic Basketball courts, two synthetic Lawn Tennis courts, two Volleyball courts, and a Handball court. The campus has impressive athletic tracks, a Cricket pitch, a Table Tennis room equipped with four tables, a Chess room, and a Skating rink. Students also have the option to build their skills in Archery, Taekwondo, Hockey, Kho-Kho, and Gymnastics.

laboratories

labs for all sciences

CHIREC has spacious and well-equipped Language Lab, Physics Lab, Chemistry Lab, Biology Lab, Biotechnology Lab, Mathematics Lab, Fashion Studies Lab, and Home Science Lab, in addition to 8 Computer Labs (including Robotics and 3D printing labs). Each laboratory is equipped to handle National and International Curricula, up to Class XII.

smart classrooms

smart technology for a smart generation

Our classrooms are large, spacious, and well-lit. These state-of-the-art, technology-enabled classrooms with ergonomic furniture offer plenty of natural ventilation. Interactive Boards have made it easier to utilize online resources, display 3D models, organize virtual field trips to different parts of the world, and create a rich learning experience for students.
